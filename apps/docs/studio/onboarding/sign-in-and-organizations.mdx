## Sign in

- Go to your Docufy workspace URL and sign in
- If invited, accept the email invitation first; your role is assigned by the inviter

## Active organization

- If you belong to multiple orgs, use the org switcher in the top/side navigation to set the active one
- The active org determines which spaces, documents, and sites you see and edit

<img src="https://izfvzcggescmuprs.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/assets/docufy/4e5ba3a2-adc0-4bfc-a4df-e70c5749324e/1ddb68cf-bdf5-4cc7-8be6-dbdc0d15b614-Untitled-10.png" />

## Roles

| Role   | Can do                                                                              |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Owner  | Billing and members, create/update/delete spaces and sites, publish, manage domains |
| Admin  | Manage members, create/update/delete spaces and sites, publish, manage domains      |
| Member | Create/edit pages and groups, organize non‑managed content                          |
