## Create documents

1. In the document tree, click New page to create a page under the current location
2. Use the ••• menu on a node to create a Group (top level) or new child pages

## Reorder and nesting

- Drag and drop to reorder (blue guideline shows drop position)
- Drop before/after a sibling to reorder; drop inside to nest under a parent
- Constraint: Groups stay at the root; API‑managed items cannot be moved

## Rename, icon, and delete

- Click a title to rename; slugs update automatically
- Pick an icon from the icon picker to visually categorize pages
- Delete removes a node and its descendants (cannot be undone)
